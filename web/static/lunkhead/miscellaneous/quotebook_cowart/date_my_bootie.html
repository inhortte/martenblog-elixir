The bitchin' 'how-to' document on dates.

Permissible ways to construct a date:

  • month-day-year.
  • day-month-year (only if the day is greater in value than the month).
  • month-year.
  • year.
  • month--.
  • -day-.
  • -day-year.

Permissible but ineffective ways to construct a date:

  • month day, year.
  • day month, year.
  • year day (month).
  • [day--month[&}year]
  • month :) year :( day
  • "month|@_day_..............~year~%

Unpermissible ways to contruct a date:

  • None.
