I stare at your pictures.
I reach out to touch your skin.
Tears glisten upon my cheeks.

I sit before yet another terminal missing you, my love. My uncle doses to my right in front of a blathering television. I should turn it off, surely. Sleep has been scarce. After speaking with you in your morning, [and, oh Brynn, your words touched me deeply, filled the vacuous space within me which pines eternally for you], I fell blearily into that bed for the last time for quite a while, I am sure. One of my last thoughts before blissfully becoming unconscious, was that the next time i DO sleep in that bed, it will most probably be with you when we visit my happy-go-lucky family-type-apparatus. I smile at this thought broadly.

My mother told me pointedly that she was going to awaken me early so I could accompany them to the mall. I breathed my chagrin at this notion and went on with my business [which was working on all the modifications I have appended to this expanding electronic illustration of my ardent love for you]. I plan to hang out in this house [which, although very normal as far as surburban houses go, serves as a temporary new environment: slightly refreshing] instead and hopefully be in your scintillating presence via the telephone during a few enderingly awaited moments during my afternoon. I love you. My fatigued body and mind cry to lie sleepily at your side, holding your warm form to my skin and slowly falling into a gracious slumber.

Now, dizzily, I shall retire for the night and harbour tantalizing thoughts of our life together... a glorious span of time which will begin in nine days. Before I do so, however, I am going to re-read your email once more. My GOD, you make me feel wonderful, even from so far away, my wonderfully tasty pineapple of desire! I cannot wait to be with you again. Je t'aime toujours.

I miss you...
Holding you close, sleeping in your arms...
I want that so badly...
I love you, Brynn.
Your fricative fishstick,
