There is no inspiration in frustration...

I miss you. I speak the very first thing that comes to my mind. I miss you. You didn't call today. I assume the Belgian telephone service just sucks to all hell. God, I love you. Do these words carry the emotion I feel deep inside of my bosom? I hope so. Because I wish to be at your side. All the rest of life is bullshit. I love you, Brynn. My tattoo bounces as I type this diatribe. My eyes glaze from the fatigue I feel. My arms ache from endless situps and pushups... they want to hold you, rest in a passion that is unnamable. GOD, I love you.

Sometimes I wonder if this is only a dream and I am hoping for some fantasy my mind has conjured. Sometimes I can touch your skin in my head, run my lithe fingers along the flesh of your arms, your shoulders, down along your chest, 'round your breasts, to your stomache. This night is horrific. I miss you. I repeat myself diligently. I suppose there is no other way, except, of course, your actually being with me to feel my emotion. Otherwise, I am a rock, an island reaching its isthmuses out to sea in search of your harbour. Oh, god, Brynn, don't you know how much I love you? Sometimes I am so afraid and sometimes I am so confident. My side is cold and lonely, wishing for your warmth to heal its solitude. I am a melodramatic fool. I love you... I repeat myself again. Perhaps I should go to sleep. Call me, my love... I miss you.

Your fossilized fishstick,
-bob
