Time is a sonnet
Simply linear and lithe
But dualistic. 		Life is a ballade
For only looking backwards
Emerge patterns. 		Death is a haiku
An anticipated norm
Nonchalant and terse. 		And love is free verse
Where beauty flows fluidly
Unhindered by form.
