Chapter 17

by Bob Shelton

(10/27/1994)

I am a man with the big ideas
This is how it seems
But seeming, my darling
That is the stitch in this veil
That separates you and me.
We were on that bus
On its way to paradise
We drove too slowly, the curtain closed
No one saw the end of that show.
Read my book which has no end.
               * GAWK *
