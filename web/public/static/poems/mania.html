Poems -

Mania
2018-06-13
Vanish from
existence - or
what passes as existence for
"them" - during a
couple of days, into
a sphere where
no-one asks who
you've been on
or off about or about
which toil tugged
you up
or down or drug
you around. No-one
tenses their eyelids in
passive judgment or
worry - and
gossip singularities
relax their bounds. It's just
you and
your machine alone.
